About Me

I am currently the Technical Marketing Specialist at Cohesive Networks, where I build technical documentation, product guides, and customer demos. I am responsible for projects ranging from outreach campaigns to training. I work on building Wordpress websites, writing marketing content, managing PR operations, leading community events, and building technology communities.

In 2017 I became an AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Associate. I'm part of the content marketing, HR, and project management teams. I hold a BSM from Tulane University and a Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) Certificate of Cloud Security Knowledge.

In a past career, I ran green building LEED certification projects. As a Certified LEED AP O+M, I helped our small team conduct commercial building energy assessments and green building project management. I am a product of Nashville, a lover of New Orleans, and a proud Chicago transplant. I adore my dog, Peanut.
